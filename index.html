About the App

Prescriptions

  • Pre-written prescriptions for GPs and doctors in Egypt and Saudi Arabia.
  • Create, share, and save custom prescriptions and protocols.

Pediatric Medical Calculator

  • Calculate correct dosages for various forms of medication using trade names or active ingredients.
  • Provides drug usage precautions and contraindications.
  • Indicates suitability for G6PD deficiency patients.

Ultra Pediatric Calculator

  • Advanced tool for calculating multiple dosages for different pediatric medications.
  • Supports quick search within "Favorites" for instant access to information.

Drug Search

  • Search thousands of drugs by trade name, generic name, or registration number.
  • Comprehensive drug information for Egyptian and Saudi drugs.
  • Diverse drug search options and up-to-date prices.
  • Alternative and similar drug suggestions.
  • Sort drugs alphabetically or by price.

Mind Maps

  • Revised emergency mind maps for quick information.

Lab Values

  • Normal values for common investigations with customizable presets.

MD Dictionary

  • Detailed explanations of medical prefixes, suffixes, and abbreviations.
  • Comprehensive encyclopedia of medical terms and abbreviations with detailed explanations.
  • Includes word origins for deeper understanding of medical terminology.

MD Web

  • Browse over 1000 trusted medical websites for the latest medical information.
  • Save favorite sites for quick access.

Drugs Database

  • Massive database containing accurate information about thousands of drugs.
  • Includes composition, package inserts, pharmaceutical forms, strengths, and other details.

Productive Mode

  • Enhance your productivity by managing tasks easily without complications.
  • Supports multitasking to ensure speed and accuracy in medical work.

Floating Mode

  • Enjoy floating windows to open multiple windows simultaneously.
  • Facilitates multitasking without needing to switch between screens.

Conditions TTT

  • Organized treatment list with multiple options suitable for various medical specialties.
  • Customize favorite cases for quick patient follow-up.

Drug Interactions

  • Check drug interactions between medications to ensure treatment safety.
  • Alerts about necessary precautions and potential effects of each drug.
